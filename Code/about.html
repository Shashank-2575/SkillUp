About SkillUp

We're on a mission to democratize career guidance through the power of artificial intelligence, making professional development accessible to everyone.

Our Story

SkillUp was founded in 2024 by a team of career counselors, AI researchers, and education technologists who saw a critical gap in accessible, personalized career guidance.

Traditional career counseling is often expensive, time-consuming, and not scalable. We believed there had to be a better way to help people navigate their career journeys.

By leveraging advanced AI and machine learning, we've created a platform that provides personalized career recommendations, development roadmaps, and continuous support to thousands of professionals worldwide.

Today, SkillUp serves users across 50+ countries, helping them discover new career paths, develop essential skills, and achieve their professional goals.
